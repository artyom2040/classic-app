Introduction

A brief summary of the Baroque era's spirit—grandeur, drama, and ornate detail.

Key Characteristics

  • Basso Continuo
  • Ornamentation

Sub-Eras

Early Baroque

Marked by the birth of opera and dramatic styles.

Middle Baroque

Development of the cantata, oratorio, and instrumental music.

Late Baroque

Complex polyphony and the peak of concerto and sonata forms.
