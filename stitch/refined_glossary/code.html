Term of the Day

Scherzo

A light, playful composition, typically in triple meter. Often appearing as the third movement in a symphony, replacing the minuet.

Tempo Italian

Meaning "cheerful" or "gay" in Italian. In music, it denotes a fast, quick, and bright tempo. It is one of the most common tempo markings, typically indicating a speed of 120-168 beats per minute (BPM).
